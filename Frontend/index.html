About KRIVVAS'25

A Spectacular Cultural Fest

KRIVVAS, a spectacular cultural fest and competition, is a dynamic initiative organized by our students' Fine Arts Club – Sona Modern Theatre.

A National Celebration of Art

More than just a stage for artistic expression, KRIVVAS serves as a vibrant melting pot where talent meets tradition, creativity converges with culture, and boundaries dissolve in the spirit of unity.

A National Celebration of Art

This grand celebration brings together students from across India, offering them a unique opportunity to showcase their artistic brilliance.

A National Celebration of Art

Build meaningful connections and immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of our nation's diverse heritage.

Beyond Performance: A Cultural Mission

Participation in KRIVVAS is more than just a performance—it's an opportunity to become cultural ambassadors, fostering inclusivity, collaboration, and the spirit of artistic exchange.

Beyond Performance: A Cultural Mission

Join us in this extraordinary experience and be a part of a movement that transcends mere competition—where passion, creativity, and culture unite to create unforgettable memories!

  • Solo Event

    Rhythmic Canvas

    Unleash your soul through movements! Rhythmic Canvas is a solo dance event where dancers mesmerize the audience with unique style and energy. Feel the music, own the stage!

    Runway Reverie

    Show off your style! Runway Reverie is a fashion extravaganza where elegance meets confidence. A dazzling display of your cutting-edge fashion and captivating designs. Walk the ramp and own the spotlight.

    Symphony Fest

    Let the music roar! Symphony Fest is the epic Battle of Bands where passion ignites performance! Show off your skills, jam with your band, and win the crowd.

    Photography

    Frozen Frames

    Preserve the beauty of now! Frozen Frames is the photography event where art meets perspective. A fusion of vision, art, and storytelling. Lock the moment in a frame!

    Cricket Carnival

    Get ready to bid! Experience the excitement of the IPL auction where strategy meets sportsmanship. Craft your approach, bid, and build your dream team in this high-stakes cricket showdown!
